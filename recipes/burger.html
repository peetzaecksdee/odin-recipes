Burger

A patty between 2 buns with lettuces

My favorite burger recipe would be This Burger CHANGED my life by Guga Foods
The patty of my preference is 80% lean and 20% fat. It gives me the most satisfaction when I eat it.

Ingredients

  • Brioche buns
  • Sauce
    • 1 part Ketchup
    • 3/4 part Mayo
    • 1/4 part Mustard
    • 1/4 part Brazilian Hot Sauce
    • 1/4 part Worcestershire Sauce
  • Grounded beef
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the onion to rings
  2. Toast the buns
  3. Spread the sauce on the buns
  4. Cook the beef
  5. Place cheese of the beef
  6. Cook the onion rings
  7. Place a bun
  8. Place the beef
  9. Place the lettuce
  10. Place the onion rings
  11. Top it with another bun
